SUSTAINABLE BY DEFAULT

1,000,000 plastic bottles are purchased every minute.

500,000 tons of coffee waste go into landfills every year.

The methane emitted from coffee waste alone adds up to the equivalent emissions of 10 million cars.

Every pair of Rens is made from 6 recycled plastic bottles 150 grams of coffee waste.

So you get all the benefits of a fully-featured, street-ready sneaker while reducing the amount of plastic pollution and methane-producing organic waste that ends up in landfills.

HEY, THAT'S US

Driven by performance and sustainable by default, Rens is a diverse group of risk-takers and freethinkers who are building a new future in footwear design.

OUR STORY

Young sneaker fanatics, Son and Jesse started Rens to make the world’s best, most comfortable everyday shoe.

Starting as a deep conversation on how devastating the consequences are of the global fashion industry, Rens vowed to only use sustainable materials.

It took 3 years to develop what they considered to be the world's best shoe, based on comfort, style, performance, and sustainability.

Today, Rens is validated by top global news publishers, over 5,000 investors, and endless 5-star customer reviews.
